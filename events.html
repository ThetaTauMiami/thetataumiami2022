Phi Delt Gate Fundraiser

Saturday, September 16 9pm - 12am

@Phi Delt Gates

Come grab a quesadilla (or two) from a couple brothers to help us fundraise for Habitat for Humanity! We will be griddling from 9pm to midnight on Saturday, September 16th or until supplies run out.

Habitat for Humanity is a global non-profit organization whose goal is give everyone a decent place to live across the 50 states and 70 countries they work in. Theta Tau helps to fundraise along side them as well as do builds - sending out our brothers to a location to help build up houses and do other work to bring the house towards completion.
