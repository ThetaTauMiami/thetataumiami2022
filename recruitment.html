Ready to Join Our Brotherhood?

Theta Tau conducts recruitment at the beginning of each fall semester. Our recruitment process consists of events that allow our chapter to evaluate candidates on their values, credentials, and overall fit in our brotherhood. These events include Information Sessions, Informal Nights, and Formal Interviews.

The Recruitment Process

You must attend AT LEAST ONE Info Session to be eligible to complete the rest of the rush process.

Mega Fair

This is your first chance to meet with brothers and show your interest in joining Theta Tau! We will have a table set up at Mega Fair to answer any questions you may have about Theta Tau or our recruiting process.

Where: Cook Field, Engineering Table 12


When: Sunday, August 28 from 11am-2pm

First Info Session: Service

Join us at our first Info Session where our main focus is our Service Pillar! During the meeting, we will outline what service means to our fraternity and various service events we have done and plan to do.

Where: Benton 207


When: Tuesday, August 30th from 7-8pm

Second Info Session: Professional Development

As a professionally engineering fraternity, we focus a lot on our careers. This info session will show the professional development events we host for our brothers and the opportunities we have from current and former brothers.

Where: Benton 207


When: Wednesday, August 31 from 7-8pm

Third Info Session: Brotherhood

Brotherhood is a big part of Theta Tau. We have many activities revolving around becoming closer as a chapter and doing fun things to get our minds of the daily school grind.

Where: Benton 207


When: Thursday, September 8th from 7-8pm
